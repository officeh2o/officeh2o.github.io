Iguassu 900 - Ice Lovers Rejoice!

This point-of-use system delivers crystal clear ice from pure water. Combining technology, design and convenience, you get water purification and ice-making capability from a single source. Reverse Osmosis technology provides great tasting, pure water, so you also get great tasting beverages and ice.

Features

  • Touch Button Controlled
  • Hot, Cold and Ambient Water
  • Hot Water/Ice Locking function with touch sensor
  • Features Reverse Osmosis Filtration
  • Single spout delivery of cold, hot or ambient water
  • Single-touch continuous dispensing
  • Produces 44 lbs. of ice per day
  • Modern Streamlined Design
  • Leak Detector

Capacities

  • Hot Tank: 1.5 gal
  • Cold Tank 5.3 gal
  • Ice Storage: 8.8 lbs
  • Ice Production: 44 lbs. per day

Good for the Earth

Office H2O coolers have been shown to reduce carbon footprint by up to 55% compared to bottled water coolers. Bottleless water coolers reduce plastic bottle contribution to landfills.
