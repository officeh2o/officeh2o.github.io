3i - The FRESH HEALTHY PURE Choice!

The World's Most Advanced Point of Use Drinking Water System! Our patented technologies coupled with a four-stage Reverse Osmosis system, stainless steel holding tank and Activated Oxygen Injection™, delivers Freshness You Can Taste!

ATS-Auto Tank Sanitization

Eliminates microbiological contamination and does away with the tedious and often overlooked task of sanitizing the holding tank.

Activated Oxygen Injection

Oxygenates the water during the ATS process, delivering FRESHNESS YOU CAN TASTE™.

Filter Replacement Notification Light

Notifies you as soon as the filter needs to be replaced by the Office H2O service team — ensuring the highest level of freshness.

Pure Touch Dispense

The ergonomic design delivers an optimum dispense height and spacious dispense area for large containers which prevents germ contamination of the dispense nozzle.

CleanContact™

Anti-microbial touchpad and surrounding surfaces manufactured and tested to protect against cross-contamination of germs within the workplace.

Dispense Area Light

Illuminates the dispense area to easily identify drip tray “bullseye” for precise dispensing — keeping the work environment clean of water spillage.

24-Hour System Monitoring

At the core of our patented technology is a microprocessor that controls and monitors activated oxygen injection, filter life, water quality, and all other system functions 24 hours a day. Only the 3i can deliver this level ofprogrammability and performance that keeps your water fresh and pure.
